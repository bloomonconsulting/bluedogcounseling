Find a therapist

Therapist directories

Many therapists listed in these directories specialize in grief, pet loss, or the human-animal bond. Use the search filters on each site to find providers with relevant experience.

Psychology Today

Large national directory. Filter by specialty, insurance, and location. Search "pet loss" or "grief" in the issues field.

Visit directory ↗

Therapy Den

Directory with a focus on inclusive, identity-affirming care. Filter by specialty and sliding scale availability.

Visit directory ↗

Good Therapy

Therapist directory with detailed profiles and information on therapeutic approaches and areas of focus.

Visit directory ↗

Open Path Collective

Reduced-fee therapy directory for individuals and families with limited income. Sessions available at $30–$80.

Veterinary professionals

Organizations and support for animal care workers

Not One More Vet (NOMV)

A nonprofit providing mental health resources and peer support specifically for veterinary professionals. Offers financial grants, a peer support network, and educational content on secondary trauma and moral stress.

Visit NOMV ↗

AVMA Wellbeing Resources

The American Veterinary Medical Association maintains a set of wellbeing resources for veterinarians and veterinary students, including information on stress, mental health, and practice sustainability.

Visit AVMA ↗

VIN Foundation

Provides support for veterinary professionals including financial assistance, mental health resources, and access to the Veterinary Information Network community.

Visit VIN Foundation ↗

Humane Society of the United States: Shelter Staff Resources

Resources for shelter and rescue staff covering euthanasia support, compassion fatigue, and workforce sustainability in animal sheltering.

Visit HSUS ↗
