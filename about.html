About Blue Dog Counseling

Grief-informed consulting, training, and workshops for veterinary practices, animal shelters, and everyone who works with animals.

The gap we address

Specialized support for the animal care workforce

The bond between humans and animals is one of the most significant connections in life. The grief, stress, and complex decisions associated with that bond, particularly in professional animal care settings, are consistently underaddressed by standard mental health training and generic workplace wellness programs.

Blue Dog Counseling was founded specifically to bridge that gap. We provide grief-informed consulting, training, workshops, and clinical services built around the actual occupational experiences of veterinarians, vet techs, shelter workers, animal control officers, and anyone else who works with animals.

Our expertise is developed specifically for the human-animal bond, animal loss, and the animal care workplace.

Founder

Cōlleen O'Brien, LMSW

Cōlleen founded Blue Dog Counseling to address a recognized gap: the absence of specialized support for the people who care for animals. She built a practice grounded in clinical expertise in the human-animal bond, euthanasia trauma, and the specific occupational pressures of veterinary and animal welfare work.

Her clinical foundation is in social work, grief-informed care, and a direct understanding of veterinary and shelter environments. Effective support for animal care professionals requires both clinical skill and field knowledge: an understanding of what these roles actually involve and what the language of the field is.

Blue Dog Counseling's consulting, training, and workshops reflect that foundation: evidence-based, operationally grounded, and built for the specific realities of animal care work.

How we work

Our approach

We start from a position of field knowledge

Animal care professionals arrive to an engagement where the significance of their work and their relationship to the animals in their care is already understood. Our consulting and clinical services are built on that foundation.

We are evidence-based

All consulting, training, and supervision is grounded in clinical best practices in grief, trauma, and human-animal bond research, developed specifically for animal care settings.

We support the whole practice

From individual animal care providers to veterinary staff and shelter leadership, we work at every level of the organization. Sustainable animal care requires sustainable people, at every role and at the organizational level.
