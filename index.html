For animal care professionals

Built specifically for the people doing this work

Veterinarians, vet techs, shelter staff, and others working in animal care face a specific category of occupational stress that requires a specialized response. Blue Dog Counseling's consulting and training is built on clinical expertise in grief, animal loss, and the human-animal bond, applied directly to the animal care workplace.

See what we offer providers
  • Moral stress and secondary trauma We address the specific clinical realities of working with animal death, euthanasia, and client grief daily.
  • Staff retention and team sustainability Practical consulting that addresses the operational factors driving turnover in veterinary and shelter settings.
  • Grief-informed client communication Training that gives your team a structured approach to supporting clients through animal death, euthanasia, and diagnosis conversations.
  • Evidence-based approach All consulting, training, and workshops are grounded in clinical best practices specific to grief, animal loss, and the animal care workplace.

The practice

Founded on a recognized gap in the field

Blue Dog Counseling was founded by Cōlleen O'Brien, LMSW, to address the lack of specialized support for people working in animal care. Blue Dog bridges the gap that standard mental health training leaves in addressing the human-animal bond, euthanasia trauma, and the occupational realities of veterinary and shelter work.
